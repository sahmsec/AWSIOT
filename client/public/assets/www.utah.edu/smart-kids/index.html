Smart Kids

Youth Experiences at the U of U

Welcome! The University of Utah offers many different learning experiences and activities for youth of all ages. If you're a parent searching for a class for your children or an educator wanting additional opportunities to help your students learn, this is the site for you!

Contact Us

If you have questions about a specific program or need an answer that you can’t find on the Web, you can contact campus departments directly.

  • Area Health Education Centers (AHEC) Programs – 801-585-9140
  • Engineering Summer Classes – 801-581-6911
  • High School University Program – 801-581-8761
  • Math Circle & Summer Programs - 801-585-9478
  • Physics Department Star Parties – 801-581-6901
  • Pioneer Theatre Company Education Programs – 801-581-6356
  • Red Butte Garden – 801-581-4747 (IRIS)
  • Science Classes & Programs – 801-581-6958
  • U. Night Program – 801-581-8761
  • Utah Museum of Fine Arts Programs – 801-581-7332
  • Utah Museum of Natural History Programs – 801-581-6927
  • Youth Education (Continuing Ed.) – 801-581-6984
